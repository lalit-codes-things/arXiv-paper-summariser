# Arxiv Research Copilot

Arxiv Research Copilot is evolving from a paper summarizer into an AI-native research operating system: a persistent research assistant that understands papers, remembers long-term research context, monitors literature, generates hypotheses, designs experiments, and helps turn papers into code.

## V6 Architecture

The V6 concept introduces an autonomous research copilot with:

- self-improving long-term memory,
- personalized research agents,
- multimodal paper understanding,
- equation, figure, graph, diagram, and table analysis,
- paper-to-code and experiment-generation workflows,
- research simulation and peer-review simulation,
- scalable orchestration and infrastructure for persistent research programs.

See the full V6 architecture document in [`docs/v6-ai-native-research-os.md`](docs/v6-ai-native-research-os.md).
